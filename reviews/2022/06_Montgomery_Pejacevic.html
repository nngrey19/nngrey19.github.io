Jessie Montgomery, Dora Pejačević

Apr 30, 2022

Jessie Montgomery and Dora Pejačević are two composers that have been receiving attention recently. Montgomery is an in-demand contemporary composer, known for Starburst, among other works. Pejačević was a Croation composer, who was active at the beginning of the 20th century. Her work has become increasingly popular in recent years.
