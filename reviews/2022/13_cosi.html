Soave sia il vento

May 22, 2022

The latest episode of the This Classical Life features Soave sia il vento (“May the wind be gentle”). More here. Full credits: Mozart: Così fan tutte, K. 588, Act I, Scene 6: No. 10, Terzettino. "Soave sia il vento" (Fiordiligi, Dorabella, Don Alfonso)

I finished Emily St. John Mandel's latest novel, Sea of Tranquility. It is a time-traveling mind-bender that riffs on the simulation hypothesis - the theory that we may live in one of many simulations created by a technologically advanced species as part of their scientific studies. The novel was engaging but it did not bring anything new to the discussion. By and large, it was an introduction to the idea. I wished it had gone a little further. Still, it ws another solid entry by this author.
