Aeneid, Handel, Shumann

June 9, 2022

I finished the Aeneid. I found the Fagles translation very readable, although the poem itself is a bit of a slog. Maybe it would be more engaging in the original language, but honestly, apart from historical interest, all the fighting, honor, and braggadocio get a bit repetitive.

There are a couple nice pieces in the latest episode of This Classical Life: Handel's Keyboard Suite No 1 in B-flat Major, HWV 434 and Robert Schumann's Szenen aus Goethes Faust, WoO 3. Pt. VII Hier ist die Aussicht frei (apparently this is the scene where Goethe ascends to heaven).
