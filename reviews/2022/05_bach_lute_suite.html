Bach, Lute Suite in E Major, III. Gavotte en rondeau

Apr 24, 2022

The NPR Music newsletter recommended an album of Bach compositions arranged for guitar by Jason Vieaux. The Lute Suite's Gavotte en rondeau was particularly memorable. Here is another interpretation by Frédéric Zigante - Lute Suite in E Major, BWV 1006a (Arr. for Guitar) III. Gavotte en rondeau.
