Agrippina and Nero

June 4, 2021

This week I kept running across items related to Nero.

An episode of Aria Code featured "Voi che udite il mio lamento" from Handel's opera Agrippina (HWV 6). Iestyn Davies sings the role of Ottone.

A little later, I listened to Tiranno, a new album from Kate Lindsey that features works based on Nero's life, including Handel's cantata Agrippina (HWV 110).

I have also been reading posts about the British Museum's exhibit on Nero.

Finally, there is this article in the New Yorker.

This Nero frenzy inspired me to buy a copy of Mary Beard's SPQR, which I am eagerly awaiting.
