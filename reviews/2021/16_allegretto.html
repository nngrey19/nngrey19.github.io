Beethoven's Allegretto

July 25, 2021

It is Sunday afternoon and I am listening to All Classical and reading Wolf Hall. Beethoven's Allegretto (Symphony No. 7, 2nd Movement) caught my ear. What a powerful, engaging piece. An earworm, for sure. Trivia: the Allegretto was featured in the movie The King's Speech, 2010.
