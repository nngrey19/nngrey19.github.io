Introduction to Ancient Greek History, Donald Kagan

July 12, 2021

I am listening to course lectures for Yale's Introduction to Ancient Greek History taught by Donald Kagan. It is a very traditional introduction to the material. I am supplementing the lecture with readings from Ancient Greece by Thomas Martin.

I finished these lectures on July 24. I have to say that I really enjoyed them. There was a lot of material and only part of it sank in, but at least, I know have a mental outline of the period and a foundation for future explorations of its history, literature and art.
