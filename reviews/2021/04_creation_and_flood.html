Enuma Elish and Atrahasis

May 31, 2021

The second episode of Literature and History discusses too Mesopotamian myths - Enuma Elish and Atrahasis. Enuma Elish is a Babylonian creation story. The Atrahasis is an Akkadian epic that includes a creation story and a flood story. Both may date back to the 18th century BC. The Israelites may have come into contact with these stories during the Babylonian captivity. Important elements of the Old Testament were also composed during this period.
