Enmerkar and the Lord of Aratta

Aug 14, 2021

I listened to the story of Enmerkar and the Lord of Aratta, which is featured in the first episode of the Oldest Stories podcast. It is a little early to render judgement on the podcast; however, I am glad that someone is trying to highlight this material and I plan on listening to more episodes before making any assessments. This particular story was noteworthy because it offers an explanation for the invention of writing - namely, that it was too hard for the king's messenger to remember everything. The podcast lists The Electronic Text Corpus of Sumerian Literature as a resource. This seems to be an important repository of materials.
