Great Horsetail

May 31, 2020

We regularly walk along the MAC (Multnomah Athletic Club) trail in Washington Park. If you enter the trail near the Oregon Holocaust Memorial and follow it towards the Rose Garden, you will come to a fork where the trail appears to branch. The two branches run roughly parallel and, somewhat inexplicably, are both marked MAC Trail. The main branch takes you to the back side of the Rose Garden, while the unpaved branch (to the left) goes through the woods, past the garden, and eventually emerges at the Elephant House. It is along this latter branch that you will find a colony of Great Horsetails.
